In old systems, people are afraid to look at their bank accounts. The fear of a low balance, expensive fees, and insufficient funds caged the minds of many who don’t believe they can overcome the negative feedback loop. They feel guilt every time they buy something and dread the times they’re forced to check their balance.

With Budgie, all of that changes. Budgie rewards you with the money you can spend responsibly every time you open the app. With a daily budget accessible via web, smartphone, and SMS, Budgie makes it easy to save.
